# Vansha Startup Thesis

## Mission
Help families understand relationships, preserve legacy, and stay connected across generations.

## Market Position
Vansha is not a family tree app.

Vansha is a private family intelligence, legacy, and social platform.

## Initial Wedge
Indian diaspora families with global-ready expansion architecture.

## Strategic Differentiators
- Culturally intelligent relationship engine
- Collaborative family graph
- Private family social network evolution
- Family events and invitations
- Legacy preservation
- AI-ready platform foundation

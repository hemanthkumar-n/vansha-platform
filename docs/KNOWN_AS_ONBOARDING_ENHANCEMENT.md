# Known As Onboarding Enhancement

## Goal
Capture family-recognized names during onboarding and relative creation.

## Add Relative Flow

Current:
- Name
- Relationship

Enhanced:
- Legal Name (optional if unknown)
- Display Name
- Known As (Optional)
- Relationship

Examples:
Known As:
- Prasad Mama
- Bujji Tata
- Kiran Anna
- Chinna Nanna

## UX
When adding a person:

Question:
"How is this person commonly known in your family?"

Optional field:
Known As

## Benefits
- Better search
- Easier identification
- Duplicate prevention
- More natural family experience

## Future
Support multiple aliases per person and alias-based search throughout the graph and AI assistant.
